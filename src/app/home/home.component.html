We released 1.7 of the game.

  • Fixed: Removing of all Notifications with improved performance.

Patch 1.6

We released 1.6 of the game.

  • Overhauled: Roads can now be placed under a building.
  • Overhauled: Accidentally deleted and unused shop-items (like tokens) are recreated when the savegame loads if they do not exist in the inventory.
  • Overhauled: Savegames are now smaller. Sadly we needed to remove the total farm monster kill stat.
  • Overhauled: Savegames now load faster for raiders with a very high level.
  • Overhauled: Increased performance of sprite pool.
  • Added: Mining Cave. Hire some miners who will mine obsidian for you. It was fortold that the god of afterlife holds a treasure far beyond the grasps of the mortals.
  • Added: StatScreen will now update every second if it is active.
  • Added: Burned Out Seal lore description updated.
  • Added: Increased the inventory tabs to 10.
  • Added: Display the current WAIR Level in the WAIR tooltip.
  • Added: Notifications for pause and focused camera modes.
  • Fixed: Caching problems with new patches. While the CacheStorage-Capabilities of the Browsers is not widely spread enough, we resort to something similar to the random-query-hack.
  • Fixed: HealStreak-Skillbook crashes if you do not have equipped at least one BasicHeal-Skillbook. It will now pay attention to the autohit abilities.
  • Fixed: Item duplication when dragging an item over a cosmetics slot.
  • Fixed: When a Reach Farm Map Level-Prophecy is fulfilled, it won't be stored as 'completed prophecy'. This makes the savegame smaller.
  • Fixed: Camera can be moved with w,a,s,d and arrow-keys when game is paused.
  • Fixed: Penguin-ItemSet emperor-choosing bug.
  • Fixed: Raid-Counter would be bugged in some cases of activity-switching.
  • Fixed: Checking of Item-Flags was error-prone. This will mostly influence the loot of Sanctuary of the Forgotten.
  • Fixed: Soft-Resetting is disabled as long as the raid cooldown is still not finished.
  • Fixed: Character view glitches of boss-groups could lead to glitching sprites.
  • Fixed: Changed class of Triskull from Mage to Archer.

Alexander Otte, August 13th, 2018
